About the NGO

InAmigos Foundation was founded in 2020 with the vision of creating a better and more inclusive society. The organization works across India through various social initiatives and volunteer programs.


The NGO focuses on helping underprivileged communities through food distribution, education support, women empowerment, environmental campaigns, and animal welfare projects.

Ongoing Projects

Project SEVA

Distribution of food and clothes to underprivileged families and people in need.

Project BachpanShala

Providing education support and digital literacy for children from poor communities.

Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill development and financial independence.

Project Jeev

Animal welfare initiative focused on feeding and protecting stray animals.

Project Prakriti

Environmental conservation through tree plantation and awareness campaigns.

Project Vikas

Internship and skill development programs for students and youth.

Social Impact

InAmigos Foundation has impacted thousands of lives through education support, food distribution drives, women empowerment, environmental awareness campaigns, and animal welfare activities.


The NGO has distributed meals and clothes, planted thousands of trees, and provided opportunities for students through internships and skill development initiatives.
